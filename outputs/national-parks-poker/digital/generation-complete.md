## Style

The generated card drafts follow the approved reference direction:

- Flat 2D card-face artwork, not product mockups.
- Large left index column.
- Separate bottom-right inverted index area.
- Central vintage national-park poster illustration.
- Thin muted red internal rule.
- Park name printed directly over sky or clean negative space.
- No ornate border, no badges, no external product background.

## Important Review Note

These images were generated directly by the model with card text and suit marks included in the image. Because text and corner indexes are model-generated rather than typeset afterward, every card should be reviewed before production for:

- Park-name spelling.
- Rank and suit correctness.
- Bottom-right inverted index correctness.
- Unwanted extra text.
- Visual consistency across the full deck.

The 6/9 cards may look reversed in the bottom-right corner when viewed upright because that index is intentionally rotated 180 degrees like a real playing card.
