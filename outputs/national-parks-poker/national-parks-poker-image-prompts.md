# National Parks Playing Cards - Image Generation Prompts

## Global Style Baseline

Use this style consistently for every card:

- Complete flat 2D playing card face design draft.
- The model must generate the full card directly, including rank, suit, park title, subtitle, and illustration.
- Not a product mockup: no table, no external background, no perspective, no shadow, no photographed card.
- Classic national-park themed playing-card layout.
- Ivory playing-card paper.
- Large clean left index column.
- Separate bottom-right inverted index area.
- Central vintage travel-poster illustration panel.
- One thin muted red internal rule plus one very thin dark rule.
- No ornate border, no badges, no filigree, no extra decorative clutter.
- Park name and `NATIONAL PARK` printed directly over sky or clean negative space, with no title box, no banner, and no colored background panel.
- WPA-era / vintage American national park poster illustration style.
- Bold simplified shapes, screenprint texture, premium collectible poker-card feeling.
- English only. No Chinese. No watermark. No logo. No extra words.
